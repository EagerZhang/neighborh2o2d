    about*






    Neighborh2o2od project (N2o2o) is initiated by a group of graduates (2020) and alumni from D&I, Tongji University, China. N2o2o is a platform created for conversations between creators, cultural organizations and brands from different fields. Starting from a graduation show named F020 to a platform for youth culture, N2o2o provides reponses to the social issues of our times.

    We focus on individual innovators and creators and try to connect them through a loose organization. Now, the exhibition F020 is ongoing. We share the real state of creators with participants mainly through conversations, interviews and lectures.
